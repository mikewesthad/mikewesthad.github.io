Video Collapse (2013) Source Code

For this project, I treated video as a source of data. I wanted to take a short scene from a video and compress it into a single image, an abstract representation of everything that happened in the video.

I wrote custom software in C++ using the openFrameworks library to take a movie, find the shapes that change over time and splash them onto a digital canvas.

In the shake series (left column), I used "Shake" by Variable as my source material, which shows a series of dogs twirling their heads, generating a whirlwind of saliva, tongue and fur.

In the gravity series (right column), I used a scene from the movie Gravity. Repeating arcing bands of blues and whites cover the screen, representing the moving edge of Earth.
