Games as Missives (2012)

These two projects, Nascent (left) and Untitled (right), are games intended as missives to family and friends. Games are a form of communication.

Nascent, a flash game built with the Flixel library, was a letter to my parents. It is hosted online here. It is a two-player game: one player controls the female avatar with A, D and space keys, while the other player controls the male avatar with the left, right and enter keys.

Untitled was a game build with the Unity game engine. It is playable online here. The player explores a world without being able to see the whole world at once, like a rat in a maze.
