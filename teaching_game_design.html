Game Design (now)

This summer I will be teaching a two week game design camp at the School of the Art Institute's Middle School Camp (ages 11-13). It is a course that I developed in collaboration with Kayla Lewis that teaches games as a form of creative expression, rather than as simple entertainment. Students will studying experimental games and will be making their own experimental games in Stencyl and in HTML5/Javascript.

More information to follow soon.
